Source URL: https://nipponindiaim.com/
Last Updated: June 1, 2026

How to download Capital Gains or Account Statements for Nippon India Mutual Fund:

Method 1: Official Nippon India Mutual Fund Website
- Step 1: Visit the official website at https://nipponindiaim.com/.
- Step 2: Navigate to the "Investor Center" or look for the "Get Account Statements" section on the home screen.
- Step 3: Select the specific statement you need, such as "Capital Gain Statement" (or "Mail Back of Capital Gain Statement").
- Step 4: Enter your Folio Number and select the desired Period (or financial year).
- Step 5: Submit the request. The statement will be emailed immediately to your registered email address.

Method 2: Official Nippon India Mutual Fund Mobile Application
- Step 1: Download and log in to the official Nippon India Mutual Fund mobile application.
- Step 2: Tap on the "More" option located in the bottom-right corner of the menu.
- Step 3: Select the "Download Statement" or "Portfolio Statement" option.
- Step 4: Select your folio number, chosen period, and click to download the PDF directly to your device or email it to your registered address.

Method 3: Via Registrar & Transfer Agents (RTA)
- CAMS Portal: Go to the CAMS Online portal at https://www.camsonline.com. Navigate to "Services for Investors" > "Statements" > "Capital Gain / Loss Statement". Provide your PAN and registered email to receive the report.
- KFintech Portal: Go to the KFintech Investor portal at https://www.kfintech.com. Navigate to "Mutual Fund Investors" > "Statements and Reports" > "Capital Gains Statements" and authenticate to download.

Important Compliance and Security Details:
- The statements are sent exclusively to the user's registered email address for privacy and security.
- The downloaded PDF statement is password-protected. The password is typically your PAN (in CAPITAL letters) or your Date of Birth.
