## Q1 — Expense Ratio

**User query:**
> "What is the expense ratio of Nippon India Large Cap Fund – Direct Plan?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
The direct plan of Nippon India Large Cap Fund carries an expense ratio of **0.65% per annum** as disclosed on the Groww scheme page (as of April 2026). Direct plans have lower expense ratios than regular plans because no distributor commission is paid. SEBI mandates that all AMCs disclose TER on a daily basis on their websites and on AMFI's website.

🔗 Source: [Nippon India Large Cap Fund – Direct Growth | Groww](https://groww.in/mutual-funds/nippon-india-large-cap-fund-direct-growth)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026

## Q2 — Exit Load

**User query:**
> "Is there an exit load on Nippon India Small Cap Fund?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
Yes — Nippon India Small Cap Fund charges an exit load of **1% if redeemed within 1 year** from the date of allotment. No exit load is charged after 1 year. Note: lumpsum subscriptions to this fund are currently restricted; only SIP investments are being accepted.

🔗 Source: [Nippon India Small Cap Fund – Direct Growth | Groww](https://groww.in/mutual-funds/nippon-india-small-cap-fund-direct-growth)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026

## Q3 — Exit Load (Silver ETF FoF — different structure)

**User query:**
> "What is the exit load for Nippon India Silver ETF FoF?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
Nippon India Silver ETF FoF charges an exit load of **1% if redeemed or switched out within 15 days** from the date of unit allotment. After 15 days, redemption is free of any exit load. This scheme is categorised as a Commodities fund investing in units of Nippon India Silver ETF.

🔗 Source: [KIM – Nippon India Silver ETF Fund of Fund (FOF) | nipponindiaim.com](https://mf.nipponindiaim.com/UploadMarketDoc/MarketingMailerDoc/KIM-cum-Application-Form-Nippon-India-Silver-ETF-Fund-of-Fund-FOF.pdf)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026

## Q5 — Exit Load Structure (Flexi Cap — free redemption of first 10%)

**User query:**
> "If I redeem some units from Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund within 6 months, will I pay an exit load?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund allows redemption of up to **10% of your invested units within 12 months without any exit load**. For redemptions exceeding that 10% threshold within 12 months, a **1% exit load** is charged. After 12 months, all redemptions are free of exit load.

🔗 Source: [SID – Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund | nipponindiaim.com](https://mf.nipponindiaim.com/campaigns/NipponIndiaFlexiCapFund/pdf/Nippon-India-Flexicap-Fund-SID.pdf)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026

## Q6 — Riskometer

**User query:**
> "What is the riskometer level of Nippon India Growth Fund (Mid Cap)?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
Nippon India Growth Fund (Mid Cap) is rated **Very High risk** on the SEBI-mandated riskometer. The riskometer is a mandatory 5-level risk classification (Low → Moderately Low → Moderate → High → Very High) that all mutual fund schemes must display. Mid cap funds typically carry Very High risk due to higher volatility compared to large cap schemes.

🔗 Source: [Nippon India Growth (Mid Cap) Fund – Direct Growth | Groww](https://groww.in/mutual-funds/nippon-india-growth-mid-cap-fund-direct-growth)  
🔗 Riskometer explained: [SEBI Investor Education – Riskometer](https://investor.sebi.gov.in/riskometer.html)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026

## Q7 — Benchmark

**User query:**
> "What is the benchmark index of Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
The AMFI Tier I benchmark for Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund is the **Nifty 500 TRI (Total Return Index)**. TRI reflects both price movements and dividend income from constituent stocks, giving a more complete picture of benchmark returns. The benchmark is disclosed in the scheme's official factsheet and SID.

🔗 Source: [Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund – Factsheet Oct 2025 | nipponindiaim.com](https://mf.nipponindiaim.com/FundsAndPerformance/ProductNotes/NipponIndia-Flexi-Cap-Fund-Oct-2025.pdf)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026

## Q8 — How to Download Capital Gains Statement

**User query:**
> "How do I download my capital gains statement for my Nippon India investments?"

**Groww Genie answer:**
You can download your capital gains statement from **CAMS (camsonline.com)** or **KFintech (kfintech.com)** — the two RTAs (Registrar & Transfer Agents) for Nippon India Mutual Fund — using your PAN and registered email. Alternatively, if you invested via Groww, you can access it under **Account → Statements → Capital Gains** in the Groww app. The statement is free of cost and covers all realized gains for the financial year you select.

🔗 Source (guide): [How to Get Capital Gains Statement for Mutual Funds | Groww Blog](https://groww.in/blog/how-to-get-capital-gains-statement-for-mutual-fund-investments)  
🔗 Source (types of statements): [Types of Mutual Fund Statements | nipponindiaim.com](https://mf.nipponindiaim.com/investoreducation/types-of-mutual-fund-statements)
📅 Last updated from sources: June 2026
